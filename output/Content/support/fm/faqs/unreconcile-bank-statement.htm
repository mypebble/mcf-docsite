How do I unreconcile a bank statement?

It is possible to unreconcile Bank Statements.

Select Bank & Cash > Unreconcile a Bank Statement

Look for the statement to cancel and Click the radio button in the left column > View Statement > Unreconcile Statement > Unreconcile

Unreconciling items will return them to the list of bank entries waiting to be reconciled. You can then reconcile the statement again and you will get the printout of the statement.
