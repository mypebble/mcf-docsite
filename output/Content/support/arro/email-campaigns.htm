Email Campaigns

The Importance of Email Campaigns

Email Campaigns are one of the most important ways of communicating with your contacts, sharing information and asking for help.

 

Email Campaigns is where you create and view any marketing campaigns you want to send to your contacts by email. You can work on campaigns you want to send out and save these as drafts until you are ready to go with them.

Email Campaigns help you to maximise your engagement with your contacts. You can run different campaigns, for example, to help you recruit volunteers or share information and generate excitement about your project.

If you choose to send an email campaign based on an existing campaign template, the number of emails sent depends on the campaign template you select. For example, the Monthly Newsletter campaign sends one email while the Hire Our Facilities campaign sends six. If you choose to create a custom campaign, you can send up to ten emails. You choose the contacts to send the campaigns to and when more than one email is sent in a campaign you choose the timeline too. All email templates must be associated with a project before being scheduled but a custom campaign is not associated with a particular project as you may want to garner support for all projects using one campaign.

After you have run an email campaign, it is listed on the Scheduled tab. Once it has been completed, it is listed on the Completed tab.

You can preview which emails have been sent in the campaign by clicking the Expand icon alongside the campaign Name. You can edit any campaign email which has not yet been sent up until the day it is due to be sent.

The Drafts tab displays the email campaigns you have previously saved as drafts. You can work on them by clicking the draft name. You can preview which emails are scheduled to send in the campaign by clicking the Expand icon alongside the campaign Name. As well as a preview of the emails which are in the draft campaign, if you have changed your mind and no longer want to send the campaign, you can remove the draft campaign here.

Create an Email campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email.
  2. Click + New Campaign.
  3. To create a new email campaign, you can:
    • Base the campaign on an existing template in the campaign template library.
    • Create a custom campaign.

Base a new campaign on an existing campaign template

  1. Click Select on the campaign template.

    2. There are a number of campaigns to choose from. Campaigns have different functions. For example, a campaign to tell your story is different from a campaign which asks for help or one which sends out a newsletter to stakeholders.

  2. Select the project from the list of available projects and click Next.

    3. Note: If you can’t see the project and you expect to, go to Projects and ensure you have marked the project as active and have published the project Joinos page.

  3. To fill in the details of the new campaign, follow the instructions in Fill in the details of a new campaign.

Create a custom campaign

  1. Click Create custom.

    2. Note: Custom campaigns are not associated with a project.

  2. Custom campaigns display one email in the campaign by default. You can add up to ten emails in total for a custom campaign. To add an email to the custom campaign, click + Add email.

  3. To remove emails from the custom campaign, click Preview/Edit alongside the email you want to remove and then click Delete email.
  4. To fill in the details of the new campaign, follow the instructions in Fill in the details of a new campaign.

Fill in the details of a new campaign

  1. Within the Details section:
    1. All contacts are automatically selected to receive the campaign. Select Click to change to expand the contact list and create your own recipient list. You can choose to exclude individual contacts within each group that is selected if you prefer to do it this way.
    2. If there is one email in the campaign, select the Send date for the email.

      3. Note: Custom campaigns are created with one email by default but you can add emails by clicking +Add email.

    3. If there is more than one email in a campaign:
      1. Select the Email frequency
        2. Once a month Every month
        Light touch Once per week
        Standard campaign Every three days
        High-touch campaign Every day
      2. Select the send date for the First email.
  2. Within the Messages to send section, preview and edit the message text if required:
    1. Custom campaign only: If you are creating a custom email campaign, click +Add email to add emails. You can add up to ten emails.
    2. Click Preview/Edit on a message. Any existing message text is displayed on the left and the Edit email box is displayed on the right. When you create a custom campaign, there will be no information filled in initially.
    3. To add or change anything, click in the appropriate box on the right and make the additions or changes.
      1. Custom campaign email only: Amend the Subject.
      2. Amend the Header.
      3. Select a Header image.
      4. Enter or amend the Body text for the campaign email.

        5. Use the rich text editing features - B (embolden text), I (italicise text), H1 (heading 1), H2 (heading 2), # (ordered list) and (unordered list) - to lay out the text in your email as you want it to appear.

        Tip: Use C to clear the formatting.

    4. When you have finished, click Back to campaign.
  3. To save the campaign as a draft to send later, click Save Draft. The campaign is listed on Drafts tab.

    4. To schedule the campaign, click Schedule. The campaign is listed on Scheduled tab.

Amending email details within a running campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Scheduled.
  2. Click the campaign name.

    3. The Details and Messages to send screen is displayed.

  3. Click Preview/Edit to amend any messages that have not yet been sent, as required.

    4. Note: You won't be able to edit messages that have already been sent but you will be able to preview the message which was sent.

  4. Click Save Changes.

Completing a draft email campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Drafts.
  2. Click the draft name you want to complete.

    3. The Details and Messages to send screen is displayed.

  3. Amend the details and messages as required.
  4. To come back and complete this campaign later, click Save draft.

    5. To send the campaign, click Schedule.

Removing a draft email campaign

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Drafts.
  2. Click the arrow > to the left of the campaign Name.
  3. From the drop-down, click Remove campaign.
  4. Confirm that you want to remove the campaign draft by clicking Remove.

Viewing completed Campaigns

  1. Open Campaigns > Email > Completed.
  2. Click the Expand icon alongside the campaign to view the emails which have been sent in the campaign.
  3. Click Preview to view the details of each email sent.

Example Email campaigns

There are many campaigns you can choose from.

For example:

  • Spread the Word
  • Supporters’ Evening
  • Project Status Update
  • Monthly Newsletter
  • Help us Find Our Alumni
  • Discover Our Services

Tip: Check Campaigns > Email > + New Campaign for the full list available.
