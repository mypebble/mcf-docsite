How do I configure my browser to enable me to print?

Issue: I print something and get the message at the top “Preparing your report for printing. Please do not leave this page.” but nothing happens.

Chrome

  • On the top left hand side of your browser there are three horizontal black lines. Select these and go down to Settings
  • Within the Search Settings box (Top Right hand side), type the word POP
  • This will highlight "Content Settings" on the left hand side - Select this
  • Once the box appears, scroll down the window until you see POP highlighted again
  • You MUST ensure that Allow all sites to show pop-ups is selected
  • Select Done and close the settings tab off
  • Refresh your browser and try printing your report again

Internet Explorer

Click the cog wheel in top right corner for set up

Click Internet Options and choose Privacy Tab

If Pop-up blocker is enabled click Settings and add the website > https://apps.mypebble.co.uk

click Add, click Close, click OK

Mozilla Firefox

Click the menu button which is the 3 lines at the top of the window and choose Options

Or

Click the orange Firefox button at the top right of the window and choose Options > Options

This will open the following dialogue box, click on Content

Click the Exceptions button

Add the text apps.mypebble.co.uk

Click > Allow

Click > Close
