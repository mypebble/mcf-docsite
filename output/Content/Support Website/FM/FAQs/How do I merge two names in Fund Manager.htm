How do I merge two names in Fund Manager?

Issue: I have two names records in Fund Manager that belong to the same pupil; how do I consolidate them and ensure the merged record retains all transactions?

Solution: In Administration > Manage Names search for and open the name you want to retain as the Primary Record for the pupil:

  • Click the Consolidate Pupil tab and search for the name you want to merge with your Primary Contact
  • Click on the radio button to select the name as your Secondary Record
  • Click on Preview Consolidation to see a list of transactions and other items that will be affected by the consolidation
  • The list of items affected can include Receipts, Payments, Transfers, Taken Items, Phone Numbers, Addresses, Email Addresses, Joinos Codes, sQuid Cards depending on what information is associated to each contact
  • Once you have confirmed the Number Being Moved, Total Value Transferred and Number Being Discarded are correct, click Confirm to complete the consolidation
  • Click Consolidate Names

Note: Consolidating names will merge and discard the items displayed â€“ this action cannot be reversed. This will also mark the Secondary Record as Out Of Use.

The Primary Record will now show the contact information consolidated from the Secondary Record at the bottom

If you are consolidating two contacts who are both GiftAid donors, you will need to select the designated GiftAid donor for the merged record before you can create a new Claim for Donations
