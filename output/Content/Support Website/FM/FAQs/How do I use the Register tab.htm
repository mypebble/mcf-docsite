How do I use the Register tab?

The Register tab allows Users to Record attendance for various Items, Meals, Clubs, Events.

Money received for a Register Account is Receipted through Fund Manager and then Recorded as attended in the Register tab. The Register function then Reports on these Taken Items and debits the Pupils Money previously credited to that Register Account.

If you would like to add the Register tab to your Fund Manager then please contact us.

Access the Register options in the Register Tab:

To use the Register feature you must ensure that the Account that you would like to Record Attendance against has been Created or Amended in the following way:

Go to > Administration > Manage Accounts

Either Create a New Account from the bottom of the screen or Amend an existing Account by clicking the blue Account link.

The example in the screenshot is for an After School Club:

Click > Register Account

Define an Exclusion Group > This is a Group that would not be expected to pay for the Item you are going to Record Attendance against in the Register tab. You must have an Exclusion Group even if it has no Pupils in the Group, you can Name this Group anything you like, we have used the example ‘FREE’. Add any Pupils to this Group that will not be expected to pay for this Item in the usual way from the Administration > Manage Names screen.

If you need to Create a New Group you can click on the ‘Manage Groups’ to the right of the Exclusion Group dropdown box to Create this.

Click > Display on Device if you are using a Tablet to Record Attendance in your Register tab - you may need to speak with Support if you have not purchased your Tablet from us to see if it is compatible with the Register App

Attendance Group and UISFM Group boxes are for Recording Pupil meals and Universal Free School Meals - help for this can be found on a separate info sheet.

Click > Save

Recording Taken Items

To mark Attendance against a Pupil go to:

Register > Record Taken Items

Choose > The Account

Choose > The Group (if applicable)

Choose > The Class (if applicable)

Choose > The Week Commencing Date

Click > Search

By not selecting a Group or a Class you will automatically produce a whole school Report.

Click to tick >The boxes of the Pupils you wish to Record Attendance against for this Account/Group/Activity

Alter > Any Amounts that differs from the Unit Cost by clicking in the Amount box - that Pupil will then be charged that altered Amount

Ticks and Amounts are automatically Saved. To correct an error, de-tick or alter the Amount box and this will be automatically Saved.

Taken Items

To see what has been Recorded go to:

Register > Taken Items

Filter to see the Report of Recorded Items:

Choose > The Account

Choose > The Group (if applicable)

Choose > The Class (if applicable)

Choose > The Week Commencing Date

Click > Search

P - The pupil has Paid for that Item F - The pupil is receiving that Item for Free

You can see the Balance of each Pupil’s Receipts to that Account, the Record of Recorded Items for the Week and the Final Balance each Pupil has remaining from Receipts entered through Fund Manager for that Account.

Name Summary

To see individual Pupil Balances go to:

Register > Name Summary

Choose > Account (if applicable)

Choose > Group (if applicable)

Choose > Dates (if applicable)

Click > Search

The Name Report will then be produced, you can use the Filter above the Name section, Print, Send a SMS or Export to CSV so you can Open in Excel.

Statement

To see what a Pupil has been Recorded against, Receipts and Balances go to:

Register > Statement

Filter > For Name

Choose > Account

Choose > Date From and To

Click > Search

The Statement Report for the Filtered Pupil will be produced.

Receipts - shows the Total of Receipts received for that pupil assigned to the Account selected

Payments - shows the Total Payments (paid out) to that Pupil from the Account selected

Paid - Taken Items in selected Date period that need to be Paid for

Free - Taken Items that do not need to be Paid for

Balance - the difference remaining between Receipts and Payments and the Taken Items for the Account selected
