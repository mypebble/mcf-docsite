The Value of Maximising Resources
You will find that you can hire out and use your school premises or property when it’s not being used during the school day and this will have two effects - it brings in more revenue to the school and it increases the community awareness about the school and it’s values and what it can offer the community.
In addition you can also trade your services and lettings with other schools, filling in the gaps for each other and generating revenue for all of you.

The Services and Lettings module is where you can manage the portfolio of your school’s potentially profitable items and also manage the qualification / approval of individuals and organisation who request the use of your facilities.

In the Services and Lettings module, you can:

  • Record and audit the services and lettings you offer.
  • Create check lists to qualify people applying to hire or lease school facilities or services.
  • Calculate the cost of sale per item offered.
  • Market your portfolio.

You can build your services and lettings portfolio by adding items from scratch or by adding services and lettings from a number of templates (e.g. meeting room, playing field, gym) and tailoring these to match your own offering. Once you have created items in your portfolio you can add images of the facilities and terms and conditions documents, so all of the information about the items on offer is held in the same place.

How do I build a services and lettings portfolio?

When you first begin to use this module it will be empty and you are prompted to build your services and lettings portfolio. You can add items from scratch or select add them by selecting from a library of typical lettings and services and amending the details to match what your school has to offer.
