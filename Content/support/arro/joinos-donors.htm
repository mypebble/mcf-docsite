Donating through the Joinos Web Page
Altruism is a means of feeling good about yourself and at the same time knowing you are helping to make a difference to a worthy cause. In addition, the UK government rewards the altruism of donation by providing relief on the tax paid on donations to charities from UK taxpayers, thereby increasing the revenue to the charity by 25%. This costs ordinary rate taxpayers nothing. Higher rate taxpayers receive tax refunds of the difference between the rate they pay and the basic rate on the donation. Gift Aid is a win-win situation.

The Joinos Web Page is a web page where you can donate to a school or academy project easily.

Access the Joinos Web Page

You can access this web page directly from the school or academy website, or if you know the URL you can type this into the browser directly.

Joinos - Web Page Elements

The Web Page has a number of elements which help you identify the project you want to donate to.

Header

The header contains the project image, title and the first of two Donate buttons. It also includes the current amount donated along with the number of supporters.

Narrative

The narrative section of the web page contains the story behind the project, the key benefits the project will have and messages from the project’s supporters. Beneath the narrative are the second of two Donate buttons and Facebook and Twitter links for you to share the project story with a social network.

Enquiring about sponsoring a project

If you are a business organisation and would like to sponsor the project by providing financial support, click on the Enquire Online button.

Become a volunteer for the project

If you are interested in helping the project by giving some of your time to help with the project, then use the Become a Volunteer form to register your interest in doing this.

About the school

Some background information about the school.

Footer

The footer contains email addresses and telephone numbers which you may find useful.

Joinos - Make a Donation

  1. Click Donate. The Donation screen opens.

  2. Enter the Amount you want to donate.

    3. The amount defaults to £10 but you can change this to any amount you want to donate.

  3. By default, the transaction fee check box is selected so that the school will receive the full donation which you have entered in the Amount box. If you don’t want to pay the transaction fee, clear this check box.
  4. By default, Single Donation is selected for a one-off donation. If you want to make a donation which recurs monthly, select Monthly Donation.
    • If you have selected Monthly Donation, choose which date in the month you want to make the donation.
  5. Enter your Title, First Name and Last Name.
  6. Enter your postal Address. This is needed if you are a UK tax payer and want to boost your donation by 25%.
  7. Enter your Email Address. This is needed so that your donation receipt can be emailed to you.
  8. If you are a UK taxpayer, you can boost your donation by 25% by allowing the school or academy to reclaim 25% of the donation amount from HMRC. To do this, select the checkbox, I am a UK Tax Payer.

    9. By selecting I am a UK Tax Payer and entering your address you are making a legal declaration of your tax paying status.

  9. If you want to donate anonymously, select the check box I’d like my identify to be hidden from others.

    10. This means that your name won't appear on the list of donors on the Donors tab but will appear alongside the text Anonymous Donation.

  10. Click Continue.

    11. The Pay for things screen appears.

    1. Enter your card details - Card Number, Name on Card, Expiry Month, Expiry Year, and the CVV security number on the back of the card.
    2. If you want to you can save the details of the card for future use. If you want to do this, select the check box Remember my card details for future use.
    3. Select I agree to the terms and conditions of this payment in order to make payment.

      4. To read the terms and conditions, click Read Terms.

  11. Click Make Payment.

    12. If payment is successful you will see a Thank you screen like this.

    • To leave a message with your donation, enter the text in the Leave a Comment box and click Submit. Your message will be visible in the Supporters section on the web page.
    • To share your donation on Twitter, click Twitter.
    • To share your donation on Facebook, click Facebook.
