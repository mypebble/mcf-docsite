Gift Aid Reporting

Fund Manager now allows you to create reports for GiftAid transactions that meet the specifications for HMRC’s online submissions.

You can generate new GiftAid Claims within the Reports section of Fund Manager and also see your previous claims history.

Requirements for GiftAid Claims

In order for a transaction to be included in your GiftAid claim, the following fields must be selected:

  1. GiftAid flag - for a transaction to be eligible for GiftAid, the GiftAid checkbox needs to be populated. This can be selected when you are entering or amending a receipt.

  2. GiftAid Donor flag - A pupil must also be selected as a GiftAid donor in order for a transaction to be included. This can be done via the ‘Update Pupil’ screen which you can access via Administration > Manage Names and clicking on the name of the pupil you wish to update.

  3. GiftAid Donor name - In order for an eligible transaction to be included in your GiftAid claim, a designated GiftAid donor must also be selected for the pupil. This can be done via the ‘Manage GiftAid Donors’ tab within Administration > Manage Names.

New claims for donations

You will notice a new ‘GiftAid’ section under reports which includes links to ‘New Claims for Donations’ and ‘Claims History’.

 

 

Click on ‘New Claims for Donations’ to generate a new GiftAid claim report. Enter the date you would like to claim GiftAid donations up to then click ‘Preview Donations’

This will let you view all transactions from eligible GiftAid donors within the donation period.

Only transactions from pupils with a designated GiftAid donor will appear within this report.

To manage the GiftAid status and designated donors for your pupils click the ‘Manage Donors’ link. This will give you the opportunity to assign a GiftAid donor name to a pupil.

If a pupil has a name assigned they must still be flagged as a GiftAid donor in order to appear in your GiftAid submission.

Click on the pupil’s name to bring up the ‘Update Pupil’ screen and populate the ‘GiftAid Donor’ flag for all pupils you would like to include.

Once you are happy with the GiftAid Donors you want to include, return to ‘New Claims for Donations’ via the Reports tab and re-run your submission.

All transactions within the period with a GiftAid Donor and flag assigned will be visible with the option to ‘Print’, ‘Export to CSV’, ‘Close Donation Period’ and ‘Abandon’ your new claim submission.

Once you are are happy with your submission, click ‘Close Donation Period’ to finalise your submission and archive it to your Claim History.

Claim History

To view your previously submitted GiftAid claims, go to the ‘Reports’ tab and click on the ‘Claim History’ link. This will bring up details for all closed donation periods. Click on the end date link to show the report.

This will bring up the archived report for the claim period in question You will also have the option to ‘Print’ or ‘Export to CSV’ from the report screen.

Backdated GiftAid Claims

It is also possible to backdate eligible GiftAid transactions that have not been included within a donation period that is now closed.

If you update the GiftAid Donor details for a transaction that falls within a closed period it will be automatically included within your next submission.
