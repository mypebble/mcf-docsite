Consolidating Names

Highlights

Fund Manager now allows you to consolidate two pupil names and merge the associated contact details, transactions and taken items

  1. In Administration > Manage Names, search for and open the name you want to retain as the Primary Record for the pupil.

  2. Click the Consolidate Pupil tab and search for the name you want to merge with your Primary Contact.

  3. Click on the radio button to select the name as your Secondary Record.

  4. Click on Preview Consolidation to see a list of transactions and other items that will be affected by the consolidation.

    5. The list of items affected can include Receipts, Payments, Transfers, Taken Items, Phone Numbers, Addresses, Email Addresses, Joinos Codes and sQuid Cards depending on what information is associated to each contact.

  5. Once you have confirmed the Number Being Moved, Total Value Transferred and Number Being Discarded are correct, click Confirm to complete the consolidation.

    6. You will be presented with a warning that consolidating these names will merge and discard the items displayed. This action cannot be reversed.

  6. When you are happy that you are ready to continue, click Consolidate Names to complete the process.

    7. The Primary Record will now show the contact information consolidated from the Secondary Record at the bottom. The Secondary Record will be marked as Out Of Use.

    All Receipts, Payments, Transfers and Taken Items transferred from the Secondary Record will now be reported as belonging to the consolidated Primary Record.
