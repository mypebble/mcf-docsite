How do I reprint a receipt or payment?

Issue: I do not have copies of Receipt(s) / Payment(s) that I have entered, how can I Reprint them within Fund Manager?

Solution: Reprint the relevant receipt(s) or payment(s) in Fund Manager.

Method:

To print single or multiple Receipt(s) that have already been entered:

From Fund Manager > Reports > List Receipts by Receipt Number

  • Type in the relevant Receipts numbers in the From and To fields
  • Click List Receipts
  • Click the Reprint Receipts button at the bottom

To print single or multiple Payment(s) that have already been entered:

From Fund Manager > Reports > List Payments by Cheque/Voucher Number

  • Type in the relevant cheque and/or Cash Transaction numbers in the From and To fields
  • Untick Include Cheque or Include Cash Transactions if necessary
  • Click List Payments
  • Click the Print button at the bottom

If more than one payment is shown then this option will print the results as a table which does not have individual signature boxes. If you would like signature boxes for all receipts/payments then you will need to print each payment individually.
