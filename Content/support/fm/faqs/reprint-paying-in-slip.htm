How do I reprint a paying in slip?

Issue: I do not have copies of Paying In Slip(s) that I have entered, how can I Reprint them within Fund Manager?

Solution: Reprint the relevant Paying In Slip(s) or payment(s) in Fund Manager.

Method:

To print Paying In Slip(s) that have already been entered:

From Fund Manager > Bank & Cash > Bank History

  • Look for the relevant Paying In Slip number in the Reference fields
  • Click the blue Slip Number which will open the Paying In Slip
  • Click the Print button at the bottom
