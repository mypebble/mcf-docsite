How do I amend or cancel a paying in slip?

Issue: I have made a mistake on a Paying In Slip(s), how can I amend or cancel it within Fund Manager?

Solution: To amend or cancel the relevant Paying In Slip(s) or payment(s) in Fund Manager.

Method:

To amend Paying In Slip(s) that have already been entered:

  1. From Fund Manager > Bank & Cash > Cancel/Amend a Bank Transaction
  2. Look for the relevant Paying In Slip number in the Reference number fields
  3. Click the blue Slip Number which will open the Paying In Slip
  4. Make the necessary amendments to the Paying In Slip
  5. Click the Amend Paying In Slip button

Method:

To cancel Paying In Slip(s) that have already been entered:

  1. From Fund Manager > Bank & Cash > Cancel/Amend a Bank Transaction
  2. Look for the relevant Paying In Slip number in the Reference fields
  3. Click the radio button in the first column for the relevant Slip Number
  4. Click the cancel button at the bottom
  5. Confirm that this is the correct slip, click the Cancel Transaction button
