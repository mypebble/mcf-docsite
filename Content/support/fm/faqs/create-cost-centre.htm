How do I add a new account or cost centre?

Issue: I need to create a new account / cost centre to allocate/assign funds to, how do I do this?

Solution: An Account is equivalent to an individual column header in a manual ledger, and can be created at any time. There is no effective limit to the number of Accounts you may have, but each one must have its own unique Code.

Click Administration > Manage Accounts > Create New Account

  • Type Account Code and Account Name
  • Tick to Share Balances, if necessary (See section on Joinos)
  • Click Register Account, if necessary
  • Type in the Opening Balance, you may wish to transfer a balance in
  • Enter the Budget value, if necessary
  • Enter the Unit Cost, if necessary
  • Click Save

For further information regarding Creating and Managing Accounts please see our User Guide, Managing Accounts.
