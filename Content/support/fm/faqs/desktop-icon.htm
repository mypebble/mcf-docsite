How do I create a desktop icon?

Issue: I can never find my favourite/bookmark for Fund Manager, it would be great to have an icon on my desktop.

Solution: To create a browser based on my type of browser.

Chrome

click 3 lines in top right corner for set up, Click Tools / Create application shortcuts

Check or uncheck the shortcut locations (eg Remove Satart menu and Pint to Taskbar) and click Create

Internet Explorer

Click > Start > All Programs, find the Internet Explorer icon > right click > Send To > Desktop (Create Shortcut)

Locate the icon on the desktop and right click > Properties

In the Target field, click at the end of the line and type a space then https://apps.mypebble.co.uk

Click on the General Tab and change the title from Internet Explorer to Fund Manager

Click OK

Firefox

Click > Start > All Programs, find the Mozilla Firefox icon > right click > Send To > Desktop (Create Shortcut)

Locate the icon on the desktop and right click > Properties

In the Target window, click at the end of the line and type a space then https://apps.mypebble.co.uk

Click on the General Tab and change the title from Internet Explorer to Fund Manager

Click OK
