How can I see who has or hasn’t paid?

Issue: I want to be able to check an account and see who has or who hasn't’ paid.

Solution: In order to run the Who’s Not Paid reports a Unit Cost must be assigned to an account. It will also be helpful to add the Student/Staff into a Named Group to make searching easier.

To add Students and Staff to a group, please see the section To Create a New Group in the Managing Names User Guide.

For More information on Unit Cost please see the section Unit Cost in the Managing Accounts User Guide.

To see who hasn’t paid

  1. Go to Reports > Who’s Not Paid
  2. Select the Account you need to view (this will only show those accounts with Unit Costs)
  3. Select any Dates you wish to limit the search to
  4. Select either the Year, Class or Group you wish to view
  5. Click Search

    6. This then produces a list of names with their payments and Outstanding Balances

Should you also want to include in the Report all the ones who have paid in Full, then tick the box for Show Paid Balances before clicking Search.
