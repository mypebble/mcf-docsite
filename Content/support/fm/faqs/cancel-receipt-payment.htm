How do I cancel a receipt or payment?

Solution: The value of the receipt of payment needs to be removed from Fund Manager.

Do not cancel the original Cheque Receipt or alter the original Paying-in Slip.

Method:

To cancel the value of the transaction(s) entered

  1. From Fund Manager > Transactions > Cancel a Receipt of Payment Transaction
  2. Select the relevant Receipts or Payments
  3. Click Continue
  4. Type in a Reason for Cancellation
  5. Click Cancel Transactions button
