How can I transfer a negative balance from one user account to another?

Issue: I have tried to transfer a negative (overdrawn) amount from one user account to another. When I enter a '-' (minus) sign in front of the amount I get an error "You are trying to transfer a negative amount, switch the Transfer From and Transfer To if this is the intention." What do I do?

Solution: Transfer a positive amount in the opposite direction.

  • If you are still in the Transfer Screen, then switch the Transfer From and Transfer To Accounts round.
  • If you are not in the Transfer Screen
    • Transactions > Transfer Between Accounts
    • Select user accounts in the OPPOSITE sense i.e. instead of selecting the FROM account then the TO account in order to enter a negative amount
    • Enter a positive amount
    • Enter the relevant date for the Transfer

    • Enter the reason for the Transfer in the Notes Field

    • Click Transfer
