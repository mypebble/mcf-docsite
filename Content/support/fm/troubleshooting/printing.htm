I can't see my reports when I select print

As part of our latest release we’ve made some improvements to the way in which Fund Manager handles printing reports.

Google Chrome

Click 3 lines in top right corner for set up

Click Settings > Show Advanced Settings > Privacy>Content Settings > Pop-ups > Manage exceptions -

In the left box enter > apps.mypebble.co.uk and make sure 'allow' is set.

Internet Explorer

Click cog wheel in top right corner for set up

Click Privacy Tab

If Pop-up blocker is enabled click Settings and add the website > apps.mypebble.co.uk

Click Add > click Close > click OK

Mozilla Firefox

Click the menu button which is the 3 lines at the top of the window and choose Options

Or

Click the orange Firefox button at the top right of the window and choose Options > Options > Content > Exceptions

Add the text apps.mypebble.co.uk

Click Allow > Close
