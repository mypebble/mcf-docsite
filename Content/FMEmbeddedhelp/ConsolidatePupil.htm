Consolidate Pupil

About the fields Primary Record

The master pupil record which is used for the consolidation. It shows the pupil Name, Class, Year, Reg and Groups.

 List of Names

A list of pupil names is displayed, from which you choose the secondary pupil record you want to consolidate with the master pupil record.

Use this screen to search for the name you want to merge with this chosen primary contact. Consolidating involves moving receipts, payments, transfers, taken items, phone numbers, addresses and so on from the secondary to the chosen primary record which is the record at the top of this screen.

How do I ...

Consolidate?
  1. Locate the name of the pupil whose details you want to consolidate in the list of names.
  2. Click the radio button to select the pupil name as your Secondary Record.
  3. Click on Preview Consolidation to see a list of transactions that will be affected by the consolidation.

    4. The list of items affected can include Receipts, Payments, Transfers, Taken Items, Phone Numbers, Addresses, Email Addresses, Joinos Codes and sQuid Cards, depending on what information is associated with each contact.

  4. Confirm the Number Being Moved, Total Value Transferred and Number Being Discarded are correct. If they are, then click Confirm to complete the consolidation.
  5. If you are sure that you want to consolidate, click Consolidate Names at the prompt.

    6. The Primary Record will now show the contact information consolidated from the Secondary Record at the bottom. The Secondary Record is marked as Out of Use. All receipts, payments, transfers and taken items transferred from the Secondary Record are now shown as belonging to the Primary Record.
