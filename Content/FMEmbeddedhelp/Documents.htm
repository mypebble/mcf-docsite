Joinos Documents

About the fields List of Documents

The list of documents that have been uploaded is displayed, showing: document Name, whether the document was Public or not, and View attached offers.

Use this screen to view documents you have already uploaded.

How do I ...

View attached offers?
  • Locate the document in the list and click the hyperlinked Attached Offers for the document.
 Detach a document from an offer?

From here it isn’t possible to remove a document from an individual offer but you can remove it from all offers it is attached to. If you need to remove a document from a specific offer only, use Active Offers, locate the offer in the list of offers, click Amend and then remove the attached documents from that offer.

To detach a document from all offers it is attached to:

  • Locate the document in the list and click Attached Offers and then click Remove.
 Download a document from an offer?
  • Locate the document in the list and click Attached Offers and then click Download.
