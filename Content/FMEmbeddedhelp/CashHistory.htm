Cash History

About the fields Date From

The date you want to start viewing transactions.

 Date To

The date you want to stop viewing transactions.

 Search

Click Search to display a filtered list of transactions.

 List of cash transactions

By default, all cash transactions are listed, until you filter the list displayed using the Date From and Date To.

This report shows the debits and credits associated with the cash in hand account.

How do I ...

Use the search criteria?
  1. Enter the dates you want to display the results you want.
  2. Click Search.
 Print the list of transactions?
  • Click Print. A prompt tells you your report is being prepared. Once it has been prepared it is displayed as a PDF and you can then print the PDF file.
 Export the list of transactions?
  • Click Export to CSV. The list of bank transactions is automatically saved as a download CSV file.
