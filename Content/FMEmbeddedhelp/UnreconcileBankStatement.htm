Unreconcile Bank Statement

About the fields Bank Account

The bank account code and name are displayed.

 Statement Header

Details of the reconciled bank statement are shown. This includes the First Page, Last Page, Start Date, End Date, Opening Balance and Balance.

 Statement Entries

A list of entries on the statement are shown. These include Date, Entry, Reference and the Debit or Credit amounts for the entry.

Unreconciling a bank statement will return the items listed to the list of bank entries waiting to be reconciled.

How do I ...

Change the display sequence of the entries?
  • Click the hyperlinked column name to re-order the display of the entries. For example, clicking on Date will order the display in ascending or descending order. Clicking on Debit or Credit will order by amount of debit or credit.
 Unreconcile a bank statement?
  1. Enter the first and last page, the start and end date and closing balance on the statement you are reconciling.
  2. Select the Select the transactions from the list of entries to reconcile that match the transactions on your bank statement.

    3. .You can only reconcile transactions that lie between your start and end dates.

  3. Click Reconcile.
