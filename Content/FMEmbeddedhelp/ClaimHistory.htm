Claim History

About the fields List of GiftAid Claims

A list of previous gift aid claims is displayed, showing the End Date (when the claim period was closed), Total and number of Donations.

This report lets you access and view claims for gift aid made in previous closed donation periods.

How do I ...

View previously submitted GiftAid claims?
  • Click the End Date of the claim you want to view. This opens the file, displaying the donations made during the donation period.
 Print the list of claims?
  • Click Print. A prompt tells you your report is being prepared. Once it has been prepared it is displayed as a PDF and you can then print the PDF file.
 Export the list of claims?
  1. Click Export to CSV. The list of claims is automatically saved as a download CSV file.
