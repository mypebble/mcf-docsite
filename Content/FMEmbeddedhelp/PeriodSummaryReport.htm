Account summary report for period

Use this screen to review the draft account summary report for the period.

  • Section 1

    • The report shows summary income and expenditure for the period with calculated excess.

  • Section 2

    • Shows the balance brought forward into the new period and the balance carried forward from the previous period, with a summary of movement in and out during the period.

  • Section 3

    • Shows the amount owing to the fund and owed by the fund at the close of the period.

How do I ...

Print the summary report?
  • Click Print.
 Close a period?

We recommend that you don’t confirm your period end until you have previewed it and checked the figures are correct with your auditors.

  • Click Proceed to Closedown and follow the instructions on the Period End Closedown screen.
