Joinos Manage Connections

About the fields List of names

The list of names of pupils who have active connections in Joinos is displayed, showing: Name, Connections, Class, Year and Reg. To be active, the parent or carer has activated the Joinos code which they have received from you, within Joinos.

A pupil’s connections are the pupil’s parent(s) or carer(s) who are responsible for paying for their trips and activities in Joinos.

Use this screen to view the connections for pupils and remove connections that have been assigned incorrectly or that are no longer applicable.

How do I ...

Remove a connection?
  1. Locate the pupil name in the list of names.
  2. Click the pupil Name to display the connections for the name.
  3. Find the User Profile for the connection you want to remove and click Remove under Disconnect for that user.
