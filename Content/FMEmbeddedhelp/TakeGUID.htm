About the fields Pupil detail

The pupil Name, Class, Year and Reg is shown.

 List of Names

A list of names in MISapp is displayed, showing Name, Class, Year, Reg, Groups and GA (if pupil donation is eligible for claiming Gift Aid).

Use this screen if you have duplicated records for the same person. You can assign a pupil’s SIMS GUID and MISID fields to the record in Fund Manager.

How do I ...

Find Names?
  • Enter the Name you want to find (if you know it) in the Filter box.
  • Use the Filter to shorten the list of names that is displayed, by entering a character or characters you think are in the name.
 Move the pupil’s SIMS ID into Fund Manager?
  • Select the matching pupil’s name in the list of names in MISapp, then click Move SIMS ID.
