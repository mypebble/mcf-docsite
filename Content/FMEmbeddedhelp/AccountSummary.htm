Account Summary

About the fields Include Transfers

If selected, this includes transactions which show the transfer of funds between accounts so if you want to see transfers into or out of accounts, select this check box.

 Include Out of Use

If selected, this includes out of use accounts in the list of accounts displayed.

 Search

If you select Include Transfers or Include Out of Use, click Search to display accounts that include transfer transactions or are out of use.

 Filter

Enter a character or characters in the account you want to find and the list of displayed accounts adjusts accordingly to show a shorter list to choose the account from.

 List of accounts

A list of accounts in Fund Manager, showing the account Code, Account name, Receipts, Payments and Balance. Depending on your selections of Include Transfers and Include Out of Use, the list can display accounts that include transfers or are out of use. Out of use accounts are shown in the list greyed out. At the foot of the list you can see totals for Receipts, Payments and Balance.

How do I ...

View account transactions?
  • View account transactions by drilling down on the account Code to display the View Transactions screen.
 Export accounts list?
  • Export a list of the accounts to a CSV file by clicking Export CSV, choosing the location and name to save the CSV file and then clicking Save.
 Print accounts list?
  • Click Print. A prompt tells you your report is being prepared. Once it has been prepared it is displayed as a PDF and you can then print the PDF file.
 Search account balances?
  • Click Search Account Balances and follow the instructions on the Search Account Balances screen to perform a detailed search.
