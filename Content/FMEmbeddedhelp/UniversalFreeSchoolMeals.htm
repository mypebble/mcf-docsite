Universal Free School Meals

About the fields Account

The user account which school meals have been recorded against. For example, Dinners.

 Date From

The start date of the period.

 Date To

The end date of the period.

Group

The group against which free school meals are recorded is shown with the Total Taken, and then split into Free School Meals and Universal Free Meals.

Use this screen to see the total meals taken, the total number of free meals and the total number of Universal Free School Meals.

How do I ...

See the breakdown of free meals taken in a period?
  1. Select the Account.
  2. Select the Date From and Date To dates.
  3. Click Search.

    4. The Universal Free School Meals statement is shown, listing the total number of free school meals taken (Total Taken) and broken down into the number of Free school meals and Universal Free Meals.

 View what has been taken in different ways?
  • To see what items have been taken, use Register > Taken Items.
  • To see individual pupil balances, use Register > Name Summary.
  • To see item statements for individual pupils, use Register > Statement.
