Transfer between Bank Accounts

About the fields Transfer From

The bank account you want to transfer money from.

You can enter a character or characters from the bank account code in the Search field to display the bank account.

 Transfer To

The bank account you want to transfer money to.

You can enter a character or characters from the bank account code in the Search field to display the bank account.

How do I ...

Transfer between two bank accounts
  1. Select the bank account you want to transfer money from.
  2. Select the bank account you want to transfer money to.
  3. Enter the Amount to transfer.
  4. Enter or amend the transfer Date.
  5. Click Transfer.
 Transfer from the Settlement Account to another bank account
  • Select the Settlement Account to transfer money from and follow the process for transferring money between two bank accounts.
