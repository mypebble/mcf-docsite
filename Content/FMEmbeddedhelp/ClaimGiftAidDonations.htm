Claim Gift Aid Donations

About the fields List of Donations

A list of donations in the claim is displayed, showing the Ref, Donor Name, Address, Post Code, Date and Amount. The Total to Claim is also shown.

This lets you access and view the transactions in the claim for the specified period.

How do I ...

Change the display sequence of the donations?
  • Click the hyperlink column name to re-order the display. For example, clicking on Date will order the display in ascending or descending order. Clicking on Donor Name. will order alphabetically. Clicking on Amount will order by amount.
 Print the list of donations?
  • Click Print. A prompt tells you your report is being prepared. Once it has been prepared it is displayed as a PDF and you can then print the PDF file.
 Export the list of donations?
  1. Click Export to CSV. The list of claims is automatically saved as a download CSV file.
