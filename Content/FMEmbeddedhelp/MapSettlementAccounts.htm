About the fields Payment Items

A selection of your ParentPay payment items are displayed at the left hand side of the screen.

 Settlement Account

Select the Fund Manager Setllement Account which maps to the Payment Item. Ignore any unwanted bank accounts from ParentPay on this screen.

Mapping bank accounts is usually completed at Pebble Training. But if you add a further account in ParentPay and you want to see these transactions in Fund Manager you will need to map this bank account in Fund Manager. Use this screen to map your bank accounts to ensure that transactions import into the correct names and accounts in Fund Manager.

You won’t see any transactions in Fund Manager for the account until the first transaction is processed in ParentPay. There may be some accounts that you don’t need to map because the money may be going to a different bank account and so these transactions in ParentPay don’t need to be imported into Fund Manager at all.

How do I ...

Match bank accounts?
  1. Select the Fund Manager Settlement Account name to match the ParentPay selection of payment items, ignoring any accounts you don’t want to import transactions from into Fund Manager.
  2. Click Map Payment Items.
