Use help

Open help

Two levels of help are provided. High level conceptual help is accessible in the help website at mypebble.co.uk/....(give URL)

Help for each screen is available and switched on by default to display at the right hand side of the panel. You can hide or display this help by clicking the Help icon .

Ask Pebble a question

You can ask a question directly, by clicking the Help Conversation icon .

This opens a conversation window on the right hand side of the browser into which you can type your question. Our support staff will respond within four hours.
