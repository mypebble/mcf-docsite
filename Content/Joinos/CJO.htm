Joinos Offers

Parents won’t be able to buy or pay for anything in Joinos unless you create an Offer within Fund Manager and make this available to the relevant pupils.

Offers can be simple e.g. one-off with full payment up-front, or more complex e.g. recurring offers which can accept multiple payments. Some things should only be purchased once by parents e.g. a school trip, while other items, such as rugby shirts could be ordered and paid for more than once (the parent might need to buy a shirt in a bigger size when their child outgrows the one they had bought before, or the parent might want to buy their child two shirts).

To create an offer

  1. Open Fund Manager > Offers > Create Offer.
  2. Select an account and if the amount is for a third party, select Settle With Third Party.
  3. Enter the offer Title and a description, if required.
  4. Enter the cost of the offer in the Amount field.
  5. If you want to offer payment by instalments, then select Unspecified and enter the minimum amount you accept for an instalment in the Minimum box.
  6. Enter the Offer Dates and if the offer is recurring (you will offer the same thing more than once), select Recurring.
  7. Enter details about the offer quantities, the Minimum number of items each parent can purchase and the Maximum number of Items Available. If there is no limit, select Unlimited.
  8. Select any attachments that go with the offer, if there are any.

    9. If you have documents that you want to attach to the offer and they don’t show up as available then you need to upload the documents first. For more information see, To upload documents to attach to offers.

  9. Select the Names of the pupils who qualify for the offer.
  10. Click Post Offer

To share outstanding balances

You can display outstanding balances for parents who are paying by instalment on the Payments tab within the parent’s Joinos account.

Balances are only shown when parents are paying by instalments so they can check how much they still have to pay.

To do this, you need to select Share Balances on the account which is being used for the offer:

  1. Open Fund Manager > Administration > Manage Accounts.
  2. Choose the account for the offer you want to share.
  3. Ensure In Use is selected for the account.
  4. Select the Share Balances check box.

    5. This instructs Fund Manager to share all outstanding balances for offers associated with the account.

  5. Click Save.

To amend an offer that is already active

  1. Open Fund Manager > Offers > Active Offers.
  2. Find the offer you want to amend in the list and click Amend.

  3. Make the amendments you need to the offer.

    4. For example, to make the offer available to more pupils, select additional names in the Select Names list, or to extend the offer, select a new End Date that is further in the future.

  4. Click Post Offer.

To view and release pending offers

You can view and post offers that you have created and saved without posting initially.

  • Open Fund Manager > Offers > Active Offers.
  • From here you can filter the list and find the offer that’s pending, amend it or extend it.

To view and extend expired offers

You can re-open expired offers by extending the end date of the offer.

  • Open Fund Manager > Offers > Active Offers and select the Expired Offers tab.
  • From here you can filter the list and extend an expired offer by clicking Extend.

To upload documents to attach to offers

  1. Open Fund Manager > Offers > Upload Document..
  2. Enter a name and a description for the document.
  3. Choose the document by clicking Choose File and browsing to where it is on your computer network.
  4. Select Public if you want the document to be visible to all members of the school.

    5. Don’t upload any private or sensitive information. Even if you don’t select Public, the file will still be available to download.

  5. Click Upload.

To view documents you have already uploaded

  • Open Fund Manager > Offers > Documents.

    • From the list of documents you have uploaded you can see which documents are marked as public and what offers the documents have been attached to.

  • You can remove documents from offers, by clicking Attached Offers alongside the Document, selecting the offer to remove the document from and clicking Remove.
